Tuna Sandwich

Ingredients

  • Canned tune
  • Mayo
  • Sliced bread
  • Green Onions

Steps

  1. Open can of tuna and place contents into a bowl.
  2. Add 2 tbsp of mayo into bowl of tuna and mix thoroughly
  3. Finely chop green onion and add to bowl of tuna
  4. Empty bowl of tuna onto slice of bread
  5. Close the sandwich and enjoy!
